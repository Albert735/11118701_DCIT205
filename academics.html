Academic Advising

Academic advising is a collaborative and personalized process that helps students navigate their educational journey and make informed decisions about their academic and career goals. It involves a partnership between students and their advisors, who offer guidance, support, and information to enhance the overall learning experience. Academic advising goes beyond simply selecting courses; it encompasses exploring majors, understanding degree requirements, and addressing challenges that may impact academic success.
