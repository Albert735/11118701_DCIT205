Brief notes on the programs offered at the Department of Computer Science

Programmes

Undergraduate programmes

Open the door to sought-after technology carrers with a world-class Bachelor of Science in Computer Science degree.

  • This undergraduate program provides a comprehensive foundation in computer science.
  • Students gain a deep understanding of programming, algorithms, and software development.
  • The curriculum covers a range of topics including databases, networking, artificial intelligence, and more.
  • Designed to equip graduates with the skills needed for diverse careers in technology.

MPhil and Msc programmes

Embrace world class graduate education with unique blend of cutting edge researchand industry experience

  • The master's program offers advanced studies in computer science, allowing students
    to specialize in areas such as machine learning, cybersecurity, or software engineering.
  • Emphasis on research and the development of advanced technical skills.
  • Ideal for those seeking leadership roles, research positions, or specialized expertise in the field.

PhD programmes

Our PhDs lead the effort to extend the bounddaries of knowledge through quality and industry relevant research

  • The doctoral program focuses on advanced research in computer science.
  • Students work closely with faculty mentors on cutting-edge research projects.
  • Prepares graduates for roles in academia, research institutions, or as industry leaders in technological innovation.

Undergraduate degree programmes

Our doctoral programme is a full-time research programme with expected duration of 4-years. For the successful completion of this programme, a high level of commitment is required. Students are therefore expected to reside close to the University and be a full-time participant in the program. With guidance from postgraduate committee, Ph.D. students will create a personalized program of study that includes a major area of specialization and at least two minor areas of specialization. In addition to coursework, the department holds regular seminars where students and faculty review and critique research in the field. To foster prudent critique, students are expected to join at most one research lab and participate in research activities carried out by the group. The research areas of the department are Intelligent Space and Machine Learning, Data Science/Big Data Analytics, Networking and Communications Engineering, and Cyber Security
